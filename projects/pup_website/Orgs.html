Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants (JPIA)

 The academic organization for students of Bachelor of Science in Accountancy in PUPSTB.

Mision

Arming JPIANS with significant amount of knowledge and information that would help build future professionas capable of meeting the challenges of a dynamic environment in accountancy and finance industry.

Vision

To cultivate globally competitive professionals, managers, and leaders in accountancy and other associated disciplines for the finance industry.

Program Objectives

  1. To ensure the development of globally-competitive professionals, managers, and eaders in the field of accountancy and other allied disciplines through excellent, relevant, and responsive accountancy education accessible to all Filipinos, particularly to the socio-economically disadvantaged population as well as to the global community.
  2. Ascertain the generation and diffusion of knowledge and technologies in the broad range of disciplines relevant and allied to accountancy through continuing education and research, ICT networking, and linkages through national and foreign organizations.
  3. Strengthen or enhance its organizational capability to become the lead advocate for reforms and innovations and fulfill its social responsibility commitment through research and development studies, extension, and consultative services.

Psychology Society (PSYCHSOC)

 The academic organization for students of Bachelor of Science in Psychology in PUPSTB.

Department of Psychology Objectives

  1. To train students who can function as competent, committed, and compassionate psychology professionals in educational, clinical, and industrial work settings.
  2. To promote emphasis to research, extension services, and community involvement.
  3. To provide students toward scientific understanding of human behavior.
  4. To inculcate ethically and morally upright sensitive precepts in the practice of the psychology profession.

Program Objectives

  1. To promote unity among PUP students, primarily Psychology majors.
  2. To establish camaraderie regardless of program and religion
  3. To initiate campus and community-related project.
  4. To support student activities.

Computer Society (COMSOC)

 The academic organization for students of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology in PUPSTB.

Program Objectives

  • To introduce students to current technologies and tools while learning new methodologies that will lead to the development of better information systems.
  • To enable students to understand the different components of the information technology field, including hardware, software, and communication, networking, research, peopleware and management skills.
  • To demonstrate awareness of how to methodically and practically approach a variety of technological and managerial issues to ultimately improve business strategies and attain competitive damage.
  • To inculcate to students, the essensial virtues and attitudes, as well as develop necessary knowledge and competency levels required of an information technology professional.
  • To train students to systematically analyze and evaluate organizational systems and processes in order to recommend software solutions that properly address the organization's needs and goals.

Society of Electrical Engineering Students (SEES)

 The academic organization for students of BBachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering (BSEE) in PUPSTB.

Program Objectives

Within 3 to 5 years after finishing the BSEE Program, graduates:
  1. Demonstrate high level of competence in professional engineering principles, skills, and techniques in the field of Electrical Engineering and allied disciplines.
  2. Exhibit passion to lifeling learning and readiness to a constantly changing field by purusing graduate degree and continuous professional development.
  3. Show success through effective communication and professional practice of engineering or related sector by advancement through increasing responsibility and promotion in the selected field.
  4. Demonstrate active involvement in community with good leadership, integrity, and exemplary work ethics to improve society.

Association of Future Business Administrators (AFBA)

 The academic organization for students of Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship in PUPSTB.

Program Objectives

The Entrepreneurship Program aims to:
  1. Develop the students' knowledge and skills towards gob generation through the creation of profitable business/enterprise.
  2. Conduct researches on product/service that could lead to local and national development.
  3. Establish network with educational instructions, industries, GO's and NGO's, logal and international for funding sources and/or partners of researches as well as for sources of new technology.
  4. Develop faculty as competent mentors and quality researchers, through advanced studies, and other facets of continuing professional education.
  5. Strengthen the Entrepreneurship program consistent with global trend and needs.

Junior Philippine Institure of Industrial Engineers (JPIIE)

 The academic organization for students of Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering in PUPSTB.

Program Objectives

In accordance with requirements of Commission on Higher Education (CHED), the Industrial Engineering program aims to:
  1. To prepare students for a professional Industrial Engineering career including a leading role in the design, improvementm and installation of integrated systems of people, materials, information, equipment, and energy.
  2. To provide students with specialized knowledge and skills in mathematical, physical, and social sciences together with the principles and methods of engineering analysis and design to specify, predict, and evaluate the results to be obtained from such systems.
  3. To produce competitive graduates who are professionally responsible; morally, ethically, and environmentally conscious in the practice of Industrial Engineering.
