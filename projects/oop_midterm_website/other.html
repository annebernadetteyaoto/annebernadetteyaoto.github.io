Home is Where the Heart(h) Is [Poetry]

I walk inside the house
Where the light was dark
And the fire was cold

I closed my eyes
And breathed in deeply
The smell of asphodel
Overwhelmed my senses

I teared up
I miss the warmth of the fireplace
Where the fire dances brightly
Along with the shadows it produces
As the music plays a gentle, relaxing tune

I miss the flowery scent of the garden
The aroma of lilies filling the house
Making me smile with contentment and giddiness

Home
Oh how I longed going home
But here I am
In the place I once called home
Where my heart once was
Where the warmth once was
